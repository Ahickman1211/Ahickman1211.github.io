TOPEKA

Welcome to Topeka

Topeka is the state capital of Kansas. This city is located in the northeast region of the state. Topeka has a rich legacy with it being a major location during the fight for slave freedom during the US civil war. Today it is a diverse community of art and culture that still thrives today.

  • City Population: 126,587
  • Incorporated: 1857
  • Region: Northeast Kansas
  • Classification: Urban
  • Median household Income: $55,598

The Capital Of Kansas

The name Topeka is thought to be derived from the Kansa and loway tribes' languages meaning "place of dugout canoe" or to "dig good potatos."
