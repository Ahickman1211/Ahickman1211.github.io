Welcome to Kansas

The Sunflower State

Welcome to the beautiful state of Kansas. Kansas is located in the midwest region of the United States, also known as the sunflower state. Kansas is known for its rolling hills and endless wheat feilds. Kansas also holds a significant place in the history of civil rights with the Brown v. Board of Education case. With its state flag bearing the sunflower, Kansas stands as a testament to growth, resilience, and the pursuit of progress.
