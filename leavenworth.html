LEAVENWORTH

Welcome to Leavenworth

Leavenworth, Kansas also known as "The First City in Kansas"is a small city located in the northeast region of the state of Kansas. This small city sits right next to the Missouri river and is home to Fort Leavenworth and the infamous Leavenworth Federal Penetentiary. The constant stream of military members moving in and out of Leavenworth, with some choosing to stay and retire here, has created a pattern of steady growth for the city.

  • City Population: 37,351
  • Incorporated: 1855
  • Region: Northeast Kansas
  • Classification: suburban
  • Median household Income: $60,870

Downtown Leavenworth is home to many small shops and restaurants with some of the buildings downtown being older then the city itself.

There are many historical buildings in Leavenworth such as this train station which has been beautifully preserved and is now a multi-use building with many shops and restaurants inside.
